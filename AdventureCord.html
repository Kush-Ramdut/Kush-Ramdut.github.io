AdventureCord Discord Bot

AdventureCord is a turn-based RPG game played through a Discord bot of my creation. Users can create and delete characters, view their inventory, select classes, and participate in combat. The bot is built to store user data inside a database, as well as provide an engaging story and interactive combat system.
